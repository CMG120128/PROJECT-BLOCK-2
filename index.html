Introducing the solar stove

This stove is a solar stove that uses natural energy to heat food, this is a product with the objective that people that love to travel to can use this product everywhere and cook whatever they want.

The stove use mirrors to move the solar rays to a point and heat the food.

Why we do this?

Most regular houses have stoves, microwaves, and that kind of stuff. The problem with that type of things are that they use a lot of energy, practicly they use the biggest part of the energy in the houses and more energy means more contamination.

This is a way to do a stove that use natural energy.

Materials

To create this stove we use metal to do all the body of the stove. It has a mirrors that are in a speciall part of the body so they can move to any angle, they have gums on the bords soy they do not broke easy.

It also has a large metal tube in which you can put the food.
